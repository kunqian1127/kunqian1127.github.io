Thomas Bourveau


Columbia Business School




I joined Columbia University in 2018. I previously served on the faculty at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I obtained in PhD in Management Science from HEC Paris. I teach financial statement analysis in Columbia Business School's MBA program.


I primarily conducts empirical research. My research lies at the intersection of accounting, law, and economics. I am most interested in evaluating the implications of regulatory interventions in financial markets, often through the role of information disclosure.
